# Binaural Studio Project Context

## Overview

Binaural Studio is an active website and marketing experiment owned by Drew Slade.

Website: `binaural-studio.com`

The site is being developed with AI-assisted coding and is intended to test:

- AI-assisted web development.
- SEO.
- AEO and visibility in AI-generated answers.
- Content production.
- Research synthesis.
- Analytics and measurement.
- Structured publishing workflows.
- Digital products and interactive experiences.

## Current state

The project is early-stage. The website exists, but the brand, content library, and authority signals are still being built.

Current priorities include:

- Strengthening the homepage.
- Publishing baseline educational content.
- Creating supporting trust and policy pages.
- Improving technical SEO.
- Building a repeatable content workflow.
- Establishing the brand name in search results.
- Documenting the system so AI tools and collaborators can work consistently.

## Brand search experiment

One explicit marketing test is whether the site can establish the Binaural Studio brand name in search results.

At the beginning of the experiment, search results for the brand name included apps, an outdated Google Business Profile, and other results not owned by the project.

The project will document how difficult it is to:

- Establish the official website as the primary result.
- Displace irrelevant or outdated results.
- Build branded authority without a physical location.
- Earn supporting branded results through content and citations.

## Founder and author context

Drew Slade is the founder and primary author.

Relevant strengths include digital marketing, Google Ads, SEO, AEO, marketing operations, analytics, automation, AI-assisted development, and practical research synthesis.

Drew is not a physician or neuroscientist. Author positioning should be transparent. The site should not imitate clinical authority. It should establish credibility through careful research, source quality, transparent review, practical testing, and clear communication.

## Business model

The business model is still developing. Possible future models include advertising, affiliate partnerships, sponsorships, premium audio experiences, subscriptions, digital tools, courses or guides, research databases, consulting, and licensing opportunities.

Monetization should not weaken editorial trust.

## Project differentiation

Binaural Studio should avoid becoming another generic wellness website.

Differentiation should come from:

- Better explanations.
- Clear distinctions between evidence and speculation.
- Research summaries written for non-experts.
- Firsthand experiments and documented workflows.
- Transparent editorial standards.
- Original tools and structured comparisons.
- Practical listening guidance.
- Honest discussion of uncertainty and mixed results.
- A visible record of article updates and source reviews.
