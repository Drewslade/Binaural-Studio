Binaural Studio is built and run by one person — Drew — who got curious about binaural beats, went looking for a generator that didn't come wrapped in mysticism, and couldn't find one worth recommending.

The tools that existed fell into two camps: slick subscription apps that overpromised, and bare-bones tone generators with no guidance at all. Nothing in the middle treated the topic the way it deserves — as a genuinely interesting, modestly supported, completely free-to-try listening practice.

## The philosophy

Three rules govern everything on this site:

1. **The audio is honest.** The studio generates mathematically exact beats using your browser's own audio engine. No renamed MP3s, no "proprietary frequencies," no secret sauce — you can read the math on every band page.

2. **The claims are conservative.** Every use-case guide separates what's well-supported (masking, ritual, relaxation) from what's speculative (entrainment effects on performance). The research feed rates evidence strength in plain language and links to the actual studies.

3. **The tool is free and private.** Sessions run entirely on your device. No account, no tracking, no paywall between you and a 3 Hz tone, which — let's be honest — would be an absurd thing to charge for.

## Why "Studio"

Because it's a place to work with sound, not a clinic and not a temple. You bring the intent; the studio brings precise, quiet tools.
