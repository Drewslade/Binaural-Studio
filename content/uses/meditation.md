## The setup

Meditation sessions usually sit in the **theta range (4–8 Hz)**, sometimes starting in alpha and descending. In the studio, the Meditate preset (6 Hz) is the classic choice — 6 Hz is one of the few frequencies with dedicated EEG studies behind it. For a structured sit, the **Meditation Trip** journey descends from 10 Hz to 4 Hz and returns over 15 minutes, and **Theta Trance** holds a deeper 4.5 Hz plateau over 12.

## How to use it

1. Sit the way you'd normally sit — the audio is a bed, not a technique
2. Brown noise under the tone softens the electronic edge; the journeys add it automatically
3. Treat the beat like a breath anchor: something neutral to return to when the mind wanders
4. When the journey ends, the silence itself is a nice bell

## What to actually expect

A steady, featureless sound bed genuinely helps some people settle, the same way a fan or rainfall does — with the added structure of a session that has a defined arc and endpoint. Whether the 6 Hz rhythm itself deepens the state is an open research question. The sit is real either way.
