The authors reported increased theta power emerging within 10 minutes of listening, including frontal midline theta — the rhythm associated with meditative attention. It's a single lab's result with a modest sample, so treat it as encouraging rather than settled, but it's one of the few papers that measures the brain instead of just asking people how they feel."
